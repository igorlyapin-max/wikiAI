description: Build and maintain a terminal-native, orchestration-first design system for agent team HUDs, status surfaces, and control planes.
---

## Principles

- Clear hierarchy
- Compact status surfaces
- High signal-to-noise ratio
- Strong distinction between planning, execution, review, and risk states

## Colors

- Primary: core text and terminal-like surfaces
- Accent: active orchestration controls, selected workers, primary CTAs
- Success/Warning/Danger: reserved for quality gates and safety decisions

## Components

- Buttons: compact, high contrast, status-aware
- HUD cards: prioritize task state, worker state, commands, and risk
