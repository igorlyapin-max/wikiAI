## Research Policy

Use this when facts may be current, niche, version-sensitive, or external.

## Steps

1. Prefer official documentation.
2. Check dates and versions.
3. Compare at least two reliable sources when possible.
4. Separate confirmed facts from inference.
5. Cite sources in the final answer.
6. Do not use stale memory for version-sensitive facts.

## Kimi Runtime Note

For API-level web search on Kimi K2.6, prefer a no-thinking research profile when builtin web search is required.
